What Does a Debt Counsellor do for You?

Your debt counsellor will work out how much of your income should be set aside for basic living expenses, and what you can afford to pay towards your debts each month. Then they will draw up a new, more affordable repayment plan for you. Next, they will negotiate with your creditors to extend the amount of time you have to repay your debts and to have your payments reduced, so you can realistically afford them.
